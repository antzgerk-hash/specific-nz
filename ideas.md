# Specific NZ — Website Design Ideas

## Design Brief
Professional website for Specific NZ — a specialist FF&E delivery, installation, and commercial logistics company. Clients are top-end construction firms, hotel developers, fit-out managers, and corporate occupiers. The site must communicate operational credibility, project scale, and precision execution. It must never feel like a generic SME website.

## Idea 1 — Industrial Precision

**Design Movement:** Swiss International Typographic Style meets industrial editorial

**Core Principles:**
- Strict typographic grid — all content anchored to a visible underlying structure
- Contrast between raw industrial texture and clinical white space
- Data and numbers as design elements — project stats displayed at large scale
- No decorative elements — every visual element serves a structural purpose

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Near-black navy `#0D1B2A` for authority and depth
- Primary text: Pure white `#FFFFFF`
- Accent: Specific orange `#E8652A` — used sparingly for numbers, CTAs, and dividers only
- Secondary: Teal `#1A7A8A` for section labels and supporting text
- The palette communicates precision engineering, not lifestyle branding

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Asymmetric editorial grid — content never centred, always left-weighted or offset
- Full-bleed photo panels that bleed edge-to-edge with text overlaid on dark bands
- Stats sections use oversized numerals as structural elements (2,740 / 55+ / 20+)
- Navigation: fixed left sidebar on desktop, top bar on mobile

**Signature Elements:**
- Thin horizontal orange rule used as a section separator — 1px, full width
- Oversized project numbers rendered in a condensed sans-serif at 120px+
- "specific." wordmark in orange, always lowercase, always isolated with generous space

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Scroll-triggered number counters (stats animate up on viewport entry)
- Hover on project cards reveals a teal overlay with project summary
- Navigation links underline from left on hover

**Animation:**
- Entrance: sections fade up with a 40px vertical translate, 0.5s ease-out, staggered 0.1s per element
- Stats: count-up animation triggered on scroll into view
- Hero: static — no autoplay video, no carousel. One strong image, one strong headline.

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Barlow Condensed` Bold — headlines, stats, section titles
- Body: `DM Sans` Regular/Medium — paragraphs, captions, navigation
- Hierarchy: 72px display / 36px H2 / 20px H3 / 16px body / 13px caption
- Letter-spacing: +0.05em on all caps labels

## Idea 2 — Architectural Monochrome with Orange Signal

**Design Movement:** Contemporary architectural firm aesthetic — Zaha Hadid Architects / Snøhetta website style

**Core Principles:**
- Monochrome foundation with a single signal colour (orange) used as a precision accent
- Photography-first — project images are the primary content, text is secondary
- Generous whitespace communicates confidence and premium positioning
- Horizontal scrolling project gallery as a signature interaction

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Off-white `#F5F4F0` — warm, not clinical
- Text: Deep charcoal `#1C1C1C`
- Accent: Specific orange `#E8652A` — CTAs, active states, hover reveals only
- Dividers: Light warm grey `#E0DDD8`
- The palette says "we are a serious professional firm" without being cold

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-width hero with large left-aligned headline, no centring
- Services presented as a numbered vertical list with expanding detail on click
- Projects: horizontal scroll gallery on desktop, vertical stack on mobile
- About section: large team photo left, text right — editorial magazine layout

**Signature Elements:**
- Numbered service list (01. FF&E / 02. Installations / 03. Relocations / 04. Logistics)
- Orange dot used as a bullet/marker throughout — consistent micro-element
- Footer: full-width dark navy band with white text and orange wordmark

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Services expand/collapse with smooth height animation
- Project cards: image zooms slightly on hover, orange overlay fades in with project name
- CTA buttons: outline style, fill with orange on hover

**Animation:**
- Page load: headline slides in from left, 0.6s ease-out
- Service items: stagger reveal on scroll, 0.15s delay per item
- Project gallery: smooth horizontal drag/scroll with momentum

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Syne` ExtraBold — hero headlines only
- Body: `Inter` Regular/Medium — all other text
- Hierarchy: 80px hero / 40px H2 / 22px H3 / 16px body
- All section labels: uppercase, 11px, 0.15em letter-spacing, orange

## Idea 3 — Dark Authority (SELECTED)

**Design Movement:** Premium B2B industrial — think Caterpillar, Hilti, or Lendlease digital presence

**Core Principles:**
- Dark-first design communicates authority, scale, and seriousness
- Project photography used as full-bleed backgrounds with controlled dark overlays
- Typographic hierarchy is aggressive — headlines are large, bold, and left-aligned
- Every section answers a specific client question: What do you do? Who have you done it for? How big? Who are you?

**Color Philosophy:**
- Background: Deep navy `#0B1829` — the darkest point of the Specific brand
- Surface cards: `#112240` — slightly lighter navy for card backgrounds
- Primary text: White `#FFFFFF`
- Accent: Specific orange `#E8652A` — CTAs, stat numbers, active nav, hover states
- Teal: `#0D9488` — secondary accent for tags, labels, dividers
- This palette is used by the world's most credible industrial and construction brands

**Layout Paradigm:**
- Full-bleed hero: stadium/project photography with dark overlay, large left-aligned headline
- Services: 2-column asymmetric grid — icon + title left, description right
- Projects: 3-column masonry card grid with photo, project name, and sector tag
- Stats bar: full-width dark band with 4 oversized numbers (2,740 / 55+ / 20+ / 60+)
- Testimonials: horizontal scroll with client name and company
- Contact: split layout — map/address left, form right

**Signature Elements:**
- Orange left-border accent on section headings (4px solid orange left border)
- "specific." wordmark always in orange on dark backgrounds
- Stats displayed at 64px+ in orange, with white descriptor text below

**Interaction Philosophy:**
- Project cards lift with a subtle shadow increase and orange border on hover
- Navigation: sticky top bar, transparent on hero, solid navy on scroll
- CTA: solid orange button, darkens on hover

**Animation:**
- Hero headline: fade-in + 30px upward translate, 0.7s ease-out
- Stats: count-up animation on scroll into view
- Section reveals: staggered fade-up, 0.1s delay per element
- Nav: smooth background transition from transparent to navy on scroll

**Typography System:**
- Display: `Barlow Condensed` Bold/ExtraBold — all headlines, stats, hero text
- Body: `DM Sans` Regular — paragraphs, nav, captions
- Hierarchy: 80px hero / 48px H2 / 28px H3 / 16px body / 12px label
- Section labels: uppercase, 11px, 0.2em letter-spacing, teal colour

## Selected Design: **Idea 3 — Dark Authority**

Rationale: Specific NZ's clients are construction directors, hotel developers, and fit-out managers. They respond to authority and credibility signals, not lifestyle aesthetics. The dark-first palette with aggressive typography and project photography as the primary visual language positions Specific NZ at the same visual level as the major contractors they work alongside. The orange accent maintains brand consistency with the existing Specific Group identity.
